Story Summery

Chase Closed is about a detective trying to make it out in life.
He works for the NYPD in queens with his co-worker.
The crew finds themselves a murderer, who's as depraved as he is cunning.
This doesn't help with the detectives struggling mental as the murderer becomes more ambitious

Location: NY Queens, NYPD

Year: Mid-Late 2010's

Genre: Detective, Psychological fiction

Author: Muhsee Choudhury
